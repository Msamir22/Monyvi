# Egyptian Institution Logo Sources

This file records source provenance for institution logo assets used by the
mobile app. Prefer product-approved or official provider assets. Use reputable
third-party logo directories only when an official compact mark is not available
or is not usable at app-card size.

App surfaces render compact marks from `banks/marks/*` where available so the
picker, dashboard cards, and account lists stay visually consistent. InstaPay,
Central Bank of Egypt, and National Bank of Greece are intentionally excluded
from the selectable provider catalog.
